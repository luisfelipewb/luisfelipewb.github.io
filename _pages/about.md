Hi! I'm Luis, a Research Engineer at the [DREAM Lab](https://dream.georgiatech-metz.fr/) (Georgia Tech-Europe in Metz, France), where I develop, integrate, and evaluate robotic systems across a range of platforms and applications. I recently completed a PhD in Robotics jointly at Georgia Tech and Université de Lorraine, with a focus on field robotics, reinforcement learning, and computer vision. 

Before that, I spent several years in industrial R&D across Brazil and Japan, working on radar, sensors, and embedded systems for telecom and industrial applications.

My profile sits at the intersection of research and engineering. I enjoy building autonomous systems that combine perception, learning, and control. What excites me is taking cutting-edge research out of papers and simulators and making it run on real robots.

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking, traveling, and spending time outdoors. Bonus points if it involves new places or mountains.
